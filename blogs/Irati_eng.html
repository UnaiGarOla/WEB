A Weekend in Ochagavía and the Irati Forest by Van

If you are looking for a getaway full of nature, tranquility, and spectacular landscapes, spending a weekend in Ochagavía and the Irati Forest is a perfect choice. We decided to go by camper van during autumn, and the experience couldn't have been better.

Friday

Arrival in Ochagavía and an Amazing Dinner

We arrived in Ochagavía on Friday afternoon and stayed at Camping Osate with our van. It was the last week the campsite was open before closing for the season, so there were very few people. It was practically empty, which made for a very quiet atmosphere, perfect for resting.

In the evening, we went for dinner at Asador Sidrería Kixkia, one of the most famous restaurants in town. It was definitely a great choice: the food was spectacular and everything we ordered was delicious. If you visit Ochagavía, it is a highly recommended place for dinner.

Saturday

Hiking in the Irati Forest

Saturday was dedicated to hiking through the Irati Forest, one of the best-preserved beech and fir forests in Europe.

There is a small entrance fee to access the area, although we had a discount since we were staying at the campsite. Even so, it is completely worth it.

The route we took was beautiful, but we were also lucky enough to visit in autumn. The trees had stunning colors—yellows, oranges, and reds—making the landscape look like a postcard.

Additionally, we had no trouble parking. Being the low season, everything was quite peaceful, which probably changes quite a bit during the busier tourist months.

A Stroll Through the Village of Ochagavía

In the afternoon, we took the opportunity to explore the village of Ochagavía itself.

It is a small village that can be seen quite quickly, but it has a lot of charm. Its stone houses, the bridges over the river, and the peaceful atmosphere make it a perfect place for a leisurely stroll.

Sunday

Zamariain Viewpoint

On Sunday morning, we left the campsite and made one last stop at the Zamariain Viewpoint before heading back.

The views from there are incredible. The viewpoint is located on a cliff, and looking over the edge is quite thrilling due to the drop below, but at the same time, it is an impressive spot.

Furthermore, the short hike to reach the viewpoint is very easy and suitable for everyone, making it a highly recommended visit if you are in the area.

Conclusion

This weekend in Ochagavía and the Irati Forest was a perfect getaway: nature, tranquility, great food, and spectacular scenery, especially in autumn.

A highly recommended destination for a short break, whether you go by van or stay in the village.
